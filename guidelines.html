Participant Guidelines

Please read the general instructions before participating

Registration

  • Participants must register for each event separately.
  • One person can participate in multiple events.
  • Spot registrations depend on slot availability.

Reporting

  • Please report at least 15 minutes before your event.
  • Latecomers may not be allowed to participate.
  • Carrying your college ID card is mandatory.

Code of Conduct

  • Maintain discipline and follow coordinator instructions.
  • Misbehavior or unfair practices lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Respect all participants and volunteers.

Technical Req.

  • Bring necessary files, PPTs, or supporting material.
  • Do not misuse the provided systems/labs.
  • Ensure USB drives are scanned and virus-free.

General Notes

  • The Judges' decisions are final and binding.
  • Certificates will be issued to all winners and participants.
  • Ensure accurate details while filling the form.

Deadlines & Fees

  • Submission Deadline:
    26/12/2025
  • Poster Presentation:
    Rs.100/- per person
  • Other Events:
    Rs.50/- per person
