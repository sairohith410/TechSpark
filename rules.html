Event Rules

Please review the specific rules for every event

Poster Presentation

  • Individual participation only.
  • Poster must be original and not previously published.
  • Presentation: 10 mins + 2 mins Q&A.
  • Content should be concise, clear, and accurate.
  • Plagiarism > 20% leads to rejection.

Technical Quiz

  • Team size: 2–3 members.
  • Mobile phones strictly prohibited.
  • Quiz Master's decision is final.
  • Malpractice leads to immediate disqualification.
  • Multiple rounds with increasing difficulty.

Debugging & Logic Ladder

  • Team size: 2–3 members.
  • Languages: C, Java, Python.
  • No internet usage allowed.
  • Scoring based on accuracy & speed.
  • Malpractice leads to immediate disqualification.

Project Expo

  • Team size: 1–2 members.
  • Must be original and fully demonstrated.
  • Bring laptops, chargers, and materials.
  • Slides must be in PPT or PDF format.
  • Prototypes must be safe and functional, if you prepare one.
